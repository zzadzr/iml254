🕮 ANJELL'S COLLECTION 🕮

Top favourite Synopsis
Dheo's

This novel's storyline is about Dheo (Hero) who follows Nurissa Rose (Heroin), a girl who loses her memory after a tragic accident. Years later, she is forced to work for Dheo Han, a cold and mysterious man connected to her forgotten past. As hidden secrets slowly unfold, Rissa (Heroin's nickname) begins to discover the truth about herself, her family, and Dheo.
My Personal Rating: 5/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Rose's

Rose’s tells the beginning of Nurissa Rose and Dheo Han’s love story before the tragedy in Dheo’s. Coming from different worlds and beliefs, Rose and Dheo slowly fall in love while facing dangerous secrets, family conflicts, and painful sacrifices.
My Personal Rating: 4.8/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Lelaki Astatine

This novel tells the story of Astatine, a mysterious and cold-hearted man with a dark past. When love unexpectedly enters his life, he struggles between protecting the people he cares about and facing the secrets that continue to haunt him.
My Personal Rating: 4.5/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

🕮 TEME ABDULLAH'S COLLECTION 🕮

Top favourite Synopsis
Pelukis Jalanan

This is my first ever novel from Teme Abdullah. Personally, i really like this novel because the storyline very much like his self diary about his study journey. His valuable life lessons has motivates me a lot, as sometimes i can related to his hardship of study journey. That's why, this novel is really special to me.
My Personal Rating: 5/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Arkitek Jalanan

Arkitek Jalanan by Teme Abdullah continues Teme’s journey as an architecture student in London. The novel follows his struggles to balance academic pressures, personal challenges, and financial difficulties while pursuing his dream of becoming an architect. Along the way, he learns important lessons about responsibility, perseverance, friendship, and faith. Through various obstacles and experiences, Teme discovers that success requires not only talent but also patience, hard work, and trust in Allah.
My Personal Rating: 4.7/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Empayar

Empayar is a fiction novel series that follows the story of Taqif, a young man determined to build a powerful business empire despite facing numerous challenges and intense competition. As he navigates the corporate world, he encounters conflicts involving ambition, power, loyalty, and betrayal. The novel explores the sacrifices required for success and the moral dilemmas that arise when pursuing great ambitions. The series consists of four books: Empayar 1, followed by Empayar 2, Empayar 3, and the most recent installment, Empayar 4.
My Personal Rating: 4.2/5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

🕮 NORHAFSAH HAMID'S COLLECTION 🕮

Top favourite Synopsis
Will You Stay?

Will You Stay? by Norhafsah Hamid is a first every novel that i read, and it's a contemporary novel that explores themes of love, faith, healing, and personal growth. The story follows characters who face emotional struggles, difficult life choices, and challenges in their relationships. As they navigate these obstacles, they learn the importance of patience, trust, forgiveness, and reliance on Allah. The novel delivers a heartfelt message about finding strength through adversity and discovering the true meaning of love and commitment.
