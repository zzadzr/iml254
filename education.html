🏫 PRIMARY SCHOOL

I began my primary school education at the age of seven at SK Seri Gombak 1. After studying there for two years, I transferred to SK Taman Samudra as it was closer to my home. I continued my studies there until Year 6.

In addition, I was also a student at Sekolah Agama Rendah Darul Thulab Cawangan Gombak. I enrolled in the religious school when I was in Year 3 and completed my studies there alongside my primary school education.
